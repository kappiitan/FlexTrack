💡 Equipment tip: For home training, a threaded adjustable dumbbell loaded to 10–15 kg total is all you need. Microplates (0.5–1 kg increments) matter — especially for shoulder work and straight arm lifts.
🔥 Specific warm-up — Before your working sets, run through the first 1–2 exercises at reduced range/weight: 3–5 reps or short (<15s) holds, increasing range each set until you reach your working depth. Don't fatigue — you're priming, not training. These warmup sets don't count and aren't recorded.
